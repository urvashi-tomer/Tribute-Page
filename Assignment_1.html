Women In Indian Army

Notable Women

  • Gertrude Alice Ram, the military nursing service Matron-in-Chief - first woman officer in the Indian Army to attain the rank of major-general, and the first female officer in the Indian Armed Forces to attain two-star rank.
  • Priya Jhingan - first woman from Indian Army to participate in Republic Day Parade and Army Day Parade in 1994.
  • Sapper Shanti Tigga -first female jawan (private rank) in the Indian Army who joined in 2011.
  • Lieutenant Colonel Mitali Madhumita - first woman officer in India to receive a gallantry award, who received the Sena Medal in 2011 for exemplary courage shown during the attack on the Indian embassy in Kabul by terrorists in Kabul, Afghanistan on 26 February 2010, and operations in Jammu-Kashmir and the northeast states.
  • Anjana Bhaduria - first female officer in the Indian Army to win the gold medal.
  • Divya Ajith Kumar - first female officer to receive the Sword of Honor. She led all women contingent of 154 women officers and cadets during the Republic Day parade of 2015.
  • Captain Swati Singh - an engineer and then only female officer in her 63 Brigade of the Indian Army, is the first female officer to be deployed at Nathu La pass as Signals in-charge.
  • Madhuri Kanitkar - third woman to become a Lieutenant General in the Indian Army
  • Ganeve Lalji - first woman to be an attache to an Army Commander.
